About Us

Montagna Travel Montenegro is travel agency located in Nikšić. Our agency is specialized in adrenaline and outdoor sports such as cycling tours, hiking tours, canyoning tours, kayaking, trekking, via ferrata, yoga retreat as well as organising team buildings in Montenegro. We have more than 20 cycling routes marked in National Park Durmitor, National Park Lovcen, Boka Bay, National Park Skadar Lake etc. We have popular kayaking tours in Boka Bay and at Skadar Lake. 4 diffrent canyoning tours in National Park Durmitor, Kotor and Bar and starting from 1st May, First touristic 2 Via Ferratas, one in Kotor and one in National Park Durmitor.
